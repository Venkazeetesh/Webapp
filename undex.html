Jeppiaar Institute of Technology was established in 2011, to provide futuristic technical education with the perspective of innovative social and industrial application for the betterment of humanity.

Presently, the college offers five popular professional under-graduate engineering programs namely B.E. Computer Science and Engineering, B.E. Electronics and Communication Engineering, B.E.
